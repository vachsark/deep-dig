## Targeted Knowledge Recommendations

**RobloxGame software patterns**:
  - [[cs--software-architecture-patterns]] — Software architecture patterns are structural templates for organizing large-scale systems, defining how components are divided, how they communicate, where state lives, and which boundaries resist change. Each major pattern addresses a distinct axis of complexity: microservices address deployment independence and team autonomy, hexagonal architecture addresses dependency direction and testability, CQRS addresses read/write performance asymmetry, event sourcing addresses auditability and temporal queries, and domain-driven design addresses the alignment of software structure with business domains. No pattern is universally optimal; selection depends on the specific forces dominating a system -- organizational structure, data consistency requirements, latency constraints, and evolutionary changeability.
  - [[cs--open-source-software]] — Open source software is code whose source is publicly available for inspection, modification, and redistribution under licenses that permit these activities. The movement emerged in the 1980s-1990s as a reaction to proprietary software licensing; today, open source is the dominant development model in infrastructure, cloud computing, and data science, with commercial enterprises depending on thousands of open source projects they do not own.
  - [[sys--regulatory-compliance-as-a-selection-pressure-in-software-architecture]] — In evolutionary biology, natural selection is the sole directional force that builds adaptations. In SaaS architectures, the transition to regulated frameworks (such as the EU's DSA) introduces a parallel directional force. These regulations mandate specific "traits"—such as proactive risk assessments and algorithmic transparency—effectively acting as a selection pressure that filters for compliant system configurations.
